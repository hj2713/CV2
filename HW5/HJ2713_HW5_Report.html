Observation on Patterns: When denoising pure noise with an MSE loss objective, the model often outputs shapes resembling the average or "centroid" of the dataset. For MNIST, these patterns look like blurry averages of overlapping digits, since the model attempts to minimize the distance to all possible targets simultaneously.

Ablation: Removing the LR Scheduler

Compensation Strategy: To compensate for the loss of the Exponential Learning Rate scheduler, we used a smaller, constant learning rate of 1e-3 (instead of the initial 1e-2). This ensures the model does not diverge or overshoot minima later in training.
